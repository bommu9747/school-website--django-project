Apex International School
THE SCHOOL WITH INTERNATIONAL STANDARD

Apex International school is a secondary school affiliated to CBSE, Delhi, established in the year 2005. It is situated at Odumbra, in Olavanna Panchayath, Calicut. The school is located in a sprawling 3 acre green campus, 1.5 km from mini bye-pass. It is a co-educational institution caters to students from K.G to Std X. The mission of the school is to Promote Education that builds the fundamental characters such as honesty, compassion, courage, persistence and responsibility. The management envisage a completely different school with personalized care to develop intellectual and social skills. The school is an extension of family where we bring out the best out of the students with the partnership of parents. School give importance to, brotherhood, physical well-being and hygiene and inculcate civic sense among students. Training in co-curricular activities is given to every child focusing on the holistic development of the child. The school want students to enjoy every moment of their stay at school and create a love for learning and develop a sense of self esteem.
